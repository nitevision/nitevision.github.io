We are a Stockholm-based product design studio founded by former members of top Scandinavian startups including Podio, Dooer and Stardoll. We also hold experience delivering solutions for global brands such as Peak Performance, SOS Children Villages and Coca Cola.

Our secret sauce is in the profound understanding of the people we design for. Therefore, we put great attention in studying behavior, needs and expectations at dawn and throughout each step of a project.

We work closely with our clients either independently or as an integrated unit of their team. This collaborative and transparent approach allows us to maximize insight and eliminate risks in crafting user-centric digital products.
